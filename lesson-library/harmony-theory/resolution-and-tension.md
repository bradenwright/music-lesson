# Resolution and Tension in Scales

## Concept Overview
Understanding which notes in a scale create feelings of resolution (stability) versus tension (unresolved feeling) is crucial for musical expression and improvisation.

## Key Points
- Some notes in a scale feel **resolved** - they create a sense of stability and completion
- Other notes feel **unresolved** - they create tension and a desire to move to more stable notes
- This understanding helps guide improvisation and musical choices
