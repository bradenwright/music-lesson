# Bluegrass Bass Playing

## Overview
Bluegrass bass playing has specific characteristics and techniques that distinguish it from other styles.

## Core Techniques
- Emphasis on root notes, fifths, and octaves
- Simple, supportive bass lines that serve the song
- Walking bass lines (2-4 note walks between chords)
- Playing in the pocket to support the rhythm

## Learning Progression
1. Start with root notes
2. Add fifths
3. Add octaves
4. Develop simple chord walks (2-4 notes)
5. Gradually introduce more complex techniques

## Practice Approach
- Play along with recordings
- Focus on supporting the rhythm and harmony
- Develop ear for root notes and chord changes
- Practice with songs like Dark Hollow, Nellie Kane, Cold Rain and Snow

## Teaching Philosophy
- Emphasis on ear training
- Focus on pitch and tone
- Develop ability to play by ear rather than just reading
- Build jamming skills through practice
