# Scale Characteristics and Mood

## Concept Overview
Different scales and modes have distinct emotional characteristics and moods. Understanding these can help inform musical choices and expression.

## Known Examples

### Phrygian Mode
- **Characteristic**: Darker feel
- **Mood**: More somber, mysterious, or intense compared to major scales

## Key Changes

### Truck Driver Key Changes
- More obvious, direct key changes
- Common in certain styles of music
- Easier to identify and follow

### Subtle Key Changes (Jazz/Radiohead style)
- More sophisticated, less obvious transitions
- Creates different emotional effects
- Requires more developed ear to identify
